Empire of Minds

A 4X turn-based strategy game about how civilizations learn.

Vision preview: this page describes long-term design intent. The current playable build is an early prototype, and this page does not document its features in detail.

The vision

Empire of Minds is not only about climbing a fixed technology tree. Each world can shape what knowledge matters. A civilization may discover familiar paths like bronze and iron, or it may learn to thrive through stranger materials, rare resources, local adaptations, and hard-won breakthroughs. The rules remain coherent, but the path of knowledge can surprise you.

What worlds may teach

  • Each world may emphasize different resources and discoveries.
  • Familiar paths like iron and bronze may be central in one world and absent in another.
  • Other paths may matter instead — for example cultivated woods, resin-laminated materials, ceramics, or obsidian-like cutting tools. These are ideas of how play might differ, not promises of what is in any particular build.
  • The rules stay coherent, but the path of knowledge can surprise you.
  • Interesting worlds may eventually be saved and reused later.

What you can try today

The current build is an early prototype on a test map. For step-by-step instructions (founding a city, production, a temporary discovery shortcut, settlers, and a second city), open the playtest guide.
